### Implementing convolution layer in C++

Convolution is a mathematical operation on two functions f and g that produces a function $$f*g$$

$$
 (f * g)(t) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\mathcal{t})g(t-\mathcal{t}) \partial \mathcal{t}
$$
